Authenticator AppGenerate OTP using an application. No network connectivity required.

Rather than having a One Time Password (OTP) texted to you every time you Sign-In, you will use an Authenticator app on your phone to generate an OTP. You will enter the generated OTP at Sign-In the same way as with texted OTP.

  1. Open your Authenticator App. Need an app?
    Authenticator apps generate random codes that you can use to sign in. They do not have
    access to your Amazon password or account information. Amazon offers an authenticator app,
    but you can use any app you want.
    To download:
    1. Go to your phone's App store.
    2. Search for "Authenticator App".
    3. Download an app.(E.g. AWS Virtual MFA, Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator)
  2. Add an account within the app, and scan the barcode below.
    Can't scan the barcode?

    Can't scan the barcode?

    1. Open your Authenticator App and select "Manually add account" from the menu.
    2. In "Enter account name" type your full email address.
    3. In "Enter your key" type the following key (space not required):
      LVAB U3EA RX74 UPTJ EG2B UIGH BGKN LXXZ YBZS FSZJ GTRZ F7BB FERA
    4. Set key type to "Time based".
    5. Tap Add.
