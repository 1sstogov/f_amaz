Two-Step Verification (2SV) Settings

Two-Step Verification

Require a password and an OTP to Sign-In to your account

Get Started

Why do I need this?

Passwords can get stolen - especially if you use the same password for multiple sites. Adding Two-Step Verification means that even if your password gets stolen, your Amazon account will remain secure.

How does it work?

After you turn on Two-Step Verification for your account, signing in will be a little different:

  1. You'll enter your password, as usual.
  2. We'll send you a One Time Password (OTP).
  3. You'll enter the OTP, and complete your Sign-In.

On computers you use often, you can choose to suppress the OTP challenge.

What do I need to get started?

You'll need a phone with you during the enrollment process that can receive voice or text messages. We'll also show you how to set up an authenticator app on your smartphone.

To enable Two-Step Verification for your account, click "Get Started."
